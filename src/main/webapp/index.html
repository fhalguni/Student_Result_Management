KITCoEK OVERVIEW

An institute established in May 1983, KIT(Autonomous) reflects the vision of leading industrialists and educationalists. The vast exposure and experience of it’s founders has helped KIT(Autonomous) to establish it’s identity as an Institute of repute in the field of Engineering and Management education. The Institute has been awarded “A” status by Govt. of Maharashtra in recognition of its over all academic excellence and infrastructure. It is affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur and is approved by AICTE, New Delhi.The institute consists of many departments and other facilities.
